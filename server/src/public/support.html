FAQ

Is Canopy free?
Yes, completely free for attendees.

How do I save an event?
Tap any event and hit the Save button, or bookmark individual sessions in the schedule.

Why don't I see transit directions?
Transit directions require location access. Check Settings > Privacy > Location Services.

How do I get push notifications?
Save a session to your schedule and allow notifications when prompted.
